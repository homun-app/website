date: 2026-05-20

The agent can now open a **headed browser and shell** inside a sandboxed container
and do real work — and you can **watch it live** over noVNC, taking the controls back
at any moment.

- A streamed desktop, not a black box
- **Deny-by-default**: it runs only behind a permission grant
- Hand control back and forth mid-task
