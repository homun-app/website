**Effective date: 22 July 2026**

This notice explains how the public [homun.app](https://homun.app) website handles
information during a visit. It covers website analytics only; it does not cover data in
the Homun desktop app, including account data, workspaces, prompts, or files.

## Data controller

The controller is **Fabio Cantone**, responsible for the Homun project, Italy. You can
contact the controller at [hello@homun.app](mailto:hello@homun.app).

## Why we use analytics

We use privacy-oriented, aggregate analytics to understand whether the public website is
useful, which public pages are of interest, and how people interact with downloads,
GitHub resources, and the public roadmap. The legal basis is the controller's
**legitimate interest** in proportionate aggregate measurement and improvement. You have
the **right to object** to this processing.

## Information processed by Umami Cloud

Umami Cloud may process the following information for website analytics:

- Page path and title, referrer, browser language, and screen size.
- Browser, operating system, device type, site hostname, approximate country, region,
  and city, timestamps, pageviews, visits, and session duration.
- Selected events: download intent, outbound GitHub links, and opening or participating
  in a roadmap project.

The request IP address is used technically to derive an approximate country, region, and city and an anonymous session hash. Umami states that the IP address itself is **not stored**. Its analytics use a rotating salt and do not use a cookie.

## What is excluded

Website analytics do not collect Homun account data, workspace content, prompts, files,
personal names, email addresses, advertising identifiers, custom distinct IDs, or session
replay data. Query parameters and URL fragments are excluded. The analytics do not
identify visitors across websites. They do not use analytics cookies, and a browser's
Do Not Track setting disables analytics.

## Provider and international transfers

The provider is **Umami Software, Inc.**, operating Umami Cloud. Its infrastructure is in
the EU and the US. The provider's [data processing agreement](https://umami.is/umami-dpa.pdf)
describes safeguards including Standard Contractual Clauses (SCCs). See Umami's
[metric definitions](https://docs.umami.is/docs/metric-definitions) for more detail on
the reported metrics.

## Retention

Analytics are stored for no longer than **24 months**, then deleted. We review retention
regularly and use available Cloud controls or documented manual deletion to apply this
limit.

## Your rights

Where applicable, you may request access, rectification, erasure, restriction,
portability, or object to processing. Because the analytics are designed not to identify
individual visitors, we may be unable to associate a record with you without additional
information. Contact
[hello@homun.app](mailto:hello@homun.app) to exercise a right. You may also complain to
the Italian supervisory authority, the [Garante per la protezione dei dati personali](https://www.garanteprivacy.it/).

## Changes to this notice

If website analytics or this notice changes materially, we will update this notice and
its effective date.
