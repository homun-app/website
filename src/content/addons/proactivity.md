A supervisor engine watches for recurring work and surfaces **suggestion cards** — an
automation worth creating, a reply worth sending, a follow-up you'd forgotten. You stay
in control: nothing acts until you accept it.

Proactivity turns the apprentice from something you *drive* into something that
*notices* — quietly, on your machine, from the work it already observes.

- Suggestion cards you accept or dismiss
- Proposes automations from your routines
- Runs locally — no cloud watching over your shoulder
