# Homun background treatment

**Date:** 2026-07-13

## Decision

Remove the square technical grid from every marketing-page background. Replace it with restrained radial light fields that create depth without a visible repeating pattern.

## Visual rules

- no square, dot, graph-paper, or circuit pattern;
- near-black base remains unchanged;
- use at most two broad mint/deep-green radial gradients per section;
- keep contrast low enough that the background never competes with copy;
- retain technical character through panels, hairlines, mono labels, status dots, and product activity;
- reduced-motion behaviour is unaffected because the new atmosphere is static;
- inner Starlight documentation pages remain unchanged in this adjustment.

## Acceptance

The hero, ecosystem, and download sections must no longer read as graph paper at desktop or mobile sizes. They should retain visible depth, readable text, and no horizontal overflow.
